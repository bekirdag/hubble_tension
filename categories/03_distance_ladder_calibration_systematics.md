# Distance-Ladder Calibration and Local Systematics

## Solution Idea

This category treats the tension as possibly coming from the late-universe distance ladder or its
calibration. The saved papers test Cepheid crowding, metallicity, Gaia/HST calibration, Milky Way
and Magellanic Cloud anchors, TRGB, JAGB, SBF, Mira variables, Type Ia/II supernova calibration,
photometric zeropoints, and physically motivated local-ladder priors.

## Main Tradeoff

A systematic explanation is economical because it does not require new fundamental physics. It must,
however, explain why multiple local calibrator routes often remain high or why lower TRGB/JAGB/SBF
routes should be preferred.
