# Growth, S8, Weak Lensing, Clusters, and CMB Lensing

## Solution Idea

This category covers adjacent growth-of-structure evidence: S8 tension, cosmic shear, galaxy
clustering plus lensing, CMB lensing, cluster abundances, tSZ/cluster mass calibration, and
baryonic-feedback systematics. The relevance to H0 is indirect but important: many H0 solutions
change matter density, radiation content, dark energy, or growth, so they must also survive low-
redshift structure constraints.

## Main Tradeoff

These papers usually diagnose compatibility rather than solve H0 alone. A successful Hubble-tension
model should avoid worsening S8, lensing, or cluster-count tensions.
