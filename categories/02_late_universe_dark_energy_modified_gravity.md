# Late-Universe Dark Energy and Modified Gravity

## Solution Idea

This family modifies the post-recombination expansion history or the gravitational sector: evolving
dark energy, phenomenological dark-energy models, modified-gravity/unimodular/holographic
frameworks, or combined early-plus-late modifications. The goal is to alter distances at low
redshift enough to reconcile local H0 with CMB/BAO/SN constraints.

## Main Tradeoff

The main difficulty is geometric consistency. BAO plus supernovae form an inverse distance ladder
that strongly limits many late-time-only changes; several saved papers are included because they
test or criticize exactly that limitation.
