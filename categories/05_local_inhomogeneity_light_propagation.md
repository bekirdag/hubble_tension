# Local Inhomogeneity and Light-Propagation Effects

## Solution Idea

This category covers local-hole/local-void proposals and small-scale inhomogeneity or light-
propagation effects. The basic idea is that nearby density structure, calibration environment, or
non-ideal light propagation could make the locally inferred expansion rate differ from the global
one.

## Main Tradeoff

The attraction is that it uses astrophysical environment rather than new early-universe physics. The
difficulty is amplitude: saved rebuttal/no-go papers argue that realistic local structure is not
empty or coherent enough to remove the full H0 tension.
