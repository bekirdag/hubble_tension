# Independent Late-Universe H0 Probes

## Solution Idea

These papers do not usually propose a new cosmological mechanism. Instead, they try to decide the
tension with independent measurements: time-delay strong lensing, megamasers, standard sirens, dark
sirens, FRBs, supernova Refsdal, and other direct H0 constraints.

## Main Tradeoff

The strength is independence from both the CMB acoustic inference and the Cepheid/SN Ia ladder. The
challenge is controlling each probe-specific systematic, such as lens mass profiles, host-galaxy
identification, FRB baryon modeling, or gravitational-wave inclination degeneracies.
